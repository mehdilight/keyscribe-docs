# Settings

Configure KeyScribe's appearance and security options.

## Appearance

### Theme

Choose between three theme modes:

- **System** - Follows your device's dark/light mode setting
- **Dark** - Always use the dark theme (pure black background)
- **Light** - Always use the light theme

## Security

### Key Pair

View your RSA-2048 public key, fingerprint, and creation date. Share your public key via QR code, copy, or share sheet. See [Contacts & Keys](/docs/contacts) for details.

### Contacts

Manage imported public keys for encrypted note sharing. See [Contacts & Keys](/docs/contacts) for details.

### Backup & Restore

Export or import your encrypted notes with password protection. See [Backup & Restore](/docs/backup) for details.

### Biometric Unlock

When enabled, KeyScribe requires fingerprint or face recognition to open the app. This adds a layer of protection if someone has physical access to your unlocked phone.

Toggle this on or off in Settings. If your device doesn't support biometrics, this option has no effect.

## About

Shows the current app version.

## Language Support

KeyScribe supports:

- **English** - Default language
- **Arabic** - Full RTL support

The app language follows your device's language setting.
